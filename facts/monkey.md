# Monkey

- Monkeys are primates that live in many different habitats.
- There are two main groups of monkeys: New World monkeys in Central and South America and Old World monkeys in Africa and Asia.
- Monkeys are very social animals and usually live in groups.
- They communicate with a mix of facial expressions, vocal sounds, and body language.
- Many monkeys are excellent climbers and spend a large part of their lives in trees.
